Language Club Bot
It's a semester project for Data Engineering course on Theoretical Computer Science department of Jagiellonian University.

The database is used to store data about current club members, their level of knowledge of various languages and club meetings. It gives an opportunity to speed up the processes of holding a club meeting, such as registration, creation of conversations graph and administration.

Meeting procedure
1) Registration
2) Confirmation of participation exactly at the meeting
3) Face-to-face conversations with all other participants
